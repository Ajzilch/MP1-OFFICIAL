0001 Human Resource Operational Manager The Human Resource Operational Manager is responsible for ensuring the effective and efficient operation of the HR department. This includes overseeing HR processes, implementing policies and procedures, managing HR projects, and collaborating with other departments to support the organization's overall goals.

This role involves working closely with department heads, managers, and employees to ensure performance expectations align with organizational goals and objectives.

0004 Payroll Manager The Payroll Manager is responsible for overseeing the payroll function within the organization, ensuring accurate and timely processing of payroll activities. This role involves managing a team, collaborating with various departments, and maintaining compliance with relevant regulations and policies.
